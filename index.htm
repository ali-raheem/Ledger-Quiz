Question 1. Tick all that are true

A) All accounts addresses can be recovered with your seed alone.

B) To recover your accounts you need your seed and your pin.

D) Even if someone gets their hands on your Ledger they wont be able to access your account.

E) Your seed can only be used in a Ledger product

Only A) is true. Using your seed in any BIP44 compatible wallet will allow you to regerate all your accounts. Your pin is purely to secure your ledger itself. However should an adversary gain physical access to your ledger and you can not see what they have done then it should not be assumed your coins are safe.

Question 2. When you first get your Ledger you should...

A) Find your recovery sheet with in and use the seed you find on there

B) Have your Ledger generate a seed for you

D) Use the pin provided to unlock your Ledger

E) Ensure no one has scratched and revealed the seed before it reached you

Several people have lost their money to scams like this. Your ledger should arrive unintialized, there will be a series of messages then it will allow you to restore or start fresh. YOU SHOULD NOT USE A SEED UNLESS YOU ALONE SAW YOUR LEDGER GENERATE IT. Seeds should not be provided by anyone. And no one will ever ask you for your seed. Your ledger should not be set up by the seller. There should not be a filled in recovery sheet. Or a scratch and reveal recovery sheet.

Question 3. Security of your coins: which of the following are true

A) You can freely distribute your extended public key (xpub). It wont put your coins at risk

B) A single private key from a single wallet can make your entire ledger insecure

c) Together with a private key your xpub key can put your all coins on your ledger at risk

D) Your xpub and a private key will allow someone to steal all coins from a single account in single wallet due to hardened levels in the BIP44 path

Only D is true. Your XPUB will allow someone to generate all your addresses in that wallet account. This is of great a privacy concern. Together with any single private key in that account they could regerate the other private keys! Until they reach a hardened level in the derivation path. These are denoted with ' for example m/44'/0'/1'.
